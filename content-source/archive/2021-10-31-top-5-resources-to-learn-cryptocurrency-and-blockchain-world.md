## B. Books:

For people who want to learn more about blockchain technology and its applications, here are 5 of the best books on the subject.

![](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/I/81y33HrERDL.jpg)

* "**Cryptoassets**" offers an introduction to blockchain for investors, including basic concepts, a valuation framework, and practical details on getting started.

* "**Blockchain** **Revolution**" takes a look at how blockchain technology might transform the ways we live and trade in the future.

* "**The Book of Satoshi**" helps readers to understand the blockchain from the perspective of its creator, the mysterious Satoshi Nakamoto.

* "**The Basics of Bitcoins and Blockchains**" provides a comprehensive overview of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology, serving as an excellent introduction for people new to the industry.

* "**The Blockchain Developer**" is a powerful resource for those interested in digging into the technical aspects of blockchain work to start new or continue existing projects.

I have personally read Blockchain revolution and The Basics of Bitcoins and Blockchains. Other books have been recommended by my peers in this space and I am sure they would add a lot of value to your journey.

There are tons of courses out there (free and paid both) if you are serious about diving into this rabbit hole. Here are a few courses that I have personally taken on this road:

### C.1. The History of Money by Preethi Kasireddy:

Preethi is a solidity developer and has been working on multiple crypto projects. She has also launched her bootcamp program to teach solidity to newbies. She hosts a brilliant course on her [website](https://crypto.preethikasireddy.com/the-history-and-evolution-of-money/). It's free of cost and you don't have to do it all at once. This one talks about how Bitcoin even came into existence and what is the problem with existing system of money.
